Message from Hosts - UNIFREC Greece

Organizing the Five Star 3rd Annual Meeting in Athens filled us with a deep sense of joy from start to finish.

Seeing people from different places and backgrounds come together—sharing ideas, forming new connections, and strengthening existing bonds—was truly uplifting.

Athens provided the perfect backdrop for this energy: a city where conversation flows easily and inspiration feels ever-present. The laughter in the hallways, the meaningful exchanges, the spark of new collaborations—these moments reminded us why we gather each year.

More than an event, it became a celebration of community, friendship, and the power of coming together.

Katerina Areti – CEO – Unifrec – Greece
