FSFSWAN Specialist Networks

To foster increased cross-network business opportunities within our global ecosystem, we have established specialized mini groups where our forwarders with expertise in specific sectors collaborate to deliver comprehensive, end-to-end professional services tailored to their clients' requirements.

All specialist networks are free to join for FSFSWAN Members. Upon successful admission to the group, new members can apply to join any specialist network that is applicable to them at no extra cost.
