Benefits of Joining

Join one of our networks and experience the advantages of being part of a global logistics family.

Multi-Network Access

15 Specialist Networks

Access to 15 specialist networks including AOG, Breakbulk, Oil & Gas, Perishable, and Time Sensitive - all covered by your joining fee.

Payment Protection

Optional Payment Protection Programme (PPP) and FSFSWAN Secure - in-house payment protection at no extra cost.

Annual Global Summit

Automatic invitation to our annual Global Summit where all networks convene - multiple networking opportunities in one event.

Competitive Pricing

Highly competitive annual membership fee designed to deliver exceptional value for your investment.

Regional Discounts

Reduced annual membership rates available for smaller countries to ensure affordability across all regions.

Multi-Office Freedom

No additional charge for registering multiple offices within your home country.

No Hidden Fees

We do not impose any joining or administration fees - making membership accessible and straightforward.

Sole Agency Rights

Exclusive Sole Agency Status and Country-Specific Rights for your territory.

Loyalty Awards

Referral Rewards

Debt Collection Support

Fast Response
